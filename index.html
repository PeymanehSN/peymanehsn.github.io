About

I am a founder and behavioral economist working on products at the intersection of incentives, sustainability, and human decision-making. My background combines academic research, startup building, market analysis, and experimentation.

I’m especially interested in early-stage ventures where behavior, product design, and systems thinking matter. I like working on problems that need more than theory: they need validation, iteration, and a deep understanding of how people actually behave.
